About The Event

Hackers on the Hill is a semi-regular gathering for irregulars from the security research community to engage with Congressional staffers, since 2017.

The event connects cybersecurity experts with policymakers to discuss timely and relevant topics - no agendas, no talking points, just open and honest discussions.

We have built long-term relationships between dozens of staffers and hundreds of participants, as well as follow up conversations, member briefings, letters, and legislative language on topics such as:

Artificial Intelligence Critical Infrastructure Encryption Privacy Ransomware Global Adversaries Election Security Misinformation
