F.A.Q

More to come...

  • Who's in charge here?

    Hackers on the Hill is run by the all-volunteer I Am The Cavalry initiative - a group of dedicated individuals from the cybersecurity researcher community. Our goal with this event is to provide you with key resources for cybersecurity knowledge, broker connections to the wide-ranging expertise of the cybersecurity research community, and improve understanding among policymakers and the technical community.

  • When will the event start?

    An excellent question! We're still working out the details, and we intend to have a schedule posted here soon. In the meantime, please register for the event to receive updates as they become available.

  • Will there be a happy hour again?

    Happy hour? Us? ;) Yes, we're planning something similar to the happy hour we had in prior years. We'll post details here and send an email update to those who have registered as they become available.

  • What's the event like, do you have a video or write up of last year, or any prep material?

    • Watch the plenary from 2023 on our Hackers on the Hill YouTube channel.
    • Review the Guide to Meeting With Congressional Offices prepared by Harley Geiger.
    • Read our Overview of Hackers on the Hill 2023, on the I Am The Cavalry blog.

  • Will we be doing the White House this year too?

    Oh we hope so! In 2023, the White House invited a select number of hackers for an afternoon session to discuss several cybersecurity topics (and published an official White House Readout on collaborating with cybersecurity researchers!). We're hoping to repeat the trip again this year, though bear in mind that due to the very limited nature of the event (and the security precautions), this is an invite-only session.
