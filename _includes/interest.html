Hackers return to the Hill Thursday, January 11, 2024.

Look for signups to open soon - and be among the first to know when that happens by joining the notification group through the form, below.

Hackers on the Hill is a semi-regular gathering for irregulars from the security research community to engage with Congressional staffers. Run by I Am The Cavalry, the program has been bridging the gap between the hacker and public policy communities since 2017, and our sixth event was held on January 19, 2023.
